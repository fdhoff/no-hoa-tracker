Redfin → Tracker bookmarklet

One-click capture of any Redfin listing into your tracker.

Step 1 — Set your tracker URL

Where the tracker is reachable in your browser. Bookmarklets can only open http:// or https:// URLs (not file://), so run a local server:

cd /Users/foster/Projects/no-hoa-tracker && python3 -m http.server 8000

Step 2 — Drag this to your bookmarks bar

+ to No-HOA Tracker

Drag the button above into your browser's bookmarks bar. (Right-click → "Add to bookmarks" works too.)

Step 3 — Use it

  1. Open any Redfin listing detail page (e.g. redfin.com/NV/Henderson/123-Main-St/home/12345678)
  2. Click the bookmarklet
  3. The tracker opens in a new tab with the form pre-filled — review, tweak, save

Scraped fields: address, city, state, price, beds, baths, sqft, lot size, year built, MLS#, HOA status, URL. Anything missing, fill in by hand. The dedup check will warn you if you've already tracked the same MLS#.
